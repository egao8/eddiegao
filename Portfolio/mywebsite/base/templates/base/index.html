📈 (Project) AAPL Stock Trading Strategy.

Leveraging a strategy coined the Dual Moving Average Crossover (DMAC), the stock trading strategy prompts decisions for when to buy and sell a stock.

With AAPL data over the span of five years, the strategy aims to calculate two moving averages of case exchange rates of Apple stock, determine the crossover points, and ultimately output a decision on when to sell and buy.
